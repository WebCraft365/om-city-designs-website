Who we are

A Zimbabwe studio that thinks in blueprints and builds in stone.

Led by a registered Professional Architectural Technician, OM City Designs is a Professional Architectural Services and Project Management practice born and built in Harare. We combine technical precision with cutting-edge 3D visualisation to deliver council-ready residential and commercial designs across Zimbabwe.

Building in Zimbabwe has its own realities: stand sizes, council requirements, local materials and budgets that don't always appear in foreign-trained textbooks. We've spent twelve years learning those realities so you don't have to.
